About Me

Hola! I'M Michael Peter

A mathematics graduate with a passion for blending logic with creativity. Web development and design have become my playground, where I bring together my analytical skills and artistic vision to craft captivating digital experiences. Whether it's coding sleek interfaces or designing user-friendly websites, I thrive on the challenge of finding elegant solutions to complex problems.

But that's not all there is to me. I'm also a pencil artist at heart. There's something about the simplicity and precision of pencil work that draws me in. I specialize in portraits, using each stroke to capture the essence of my subjects in intricate detail. It's a process that requires patience and a keen eye for observation, but seeing a likeness emerge on the paper is incredibly rewarding.

In addition, I am a painter and mural artist, with a focus on graffiti-style and large-scale 3D wall art. Transforming blank walls into immersive, dimensional visuals is one of the most thrilling aspects of my creative journey. It allows me to merge spatial awareness, perspective, and vibrant storytelling in public spaces.

In everything I do, whether it's solving equations or bringing portraits to life, I strive to merge my mathematical mindset with my artistic sensibilities. It's this unique blend of skills and passions that defines who I am and drives me to constantly explore new avenues of expression and creation.
